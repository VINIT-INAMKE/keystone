About Us

Mechanical Engineering Student Association(MESA)

Mechanical Engineering Student Association (MESA) was established in year 2015. It plays an important role in the development of students as an engineer not only in academics but also various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Under this association, various activities were organized by MESA which aims to provide platform for all the students of Mechanical Engineering.

Vision

Provide Services which enable outstanding student experiences.

Mission

Contribute to college's mission by working in partnership with the college community to provide high quality, innovative and accessible services.

Objectives

1. Promoting the interest of students in various technical areas pertaining to Mechanical Engineering.

2. To bridge the gap between theoretical and practical field knowledge.

3. To interact with other technical societies, within and outside the institute, to promote flow of knowledge and interest.

4. To promote interaction between academics and industry by organizing industrial visits and implant training in industry.

5. To encourage novel ideas by means of special projects.

6. To provide opportunity to have lectures from external experts from academics and industry on various technical topics.
