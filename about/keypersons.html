Prof. Bharat Bhushan Sood

DEAN Training & Placements

  • Prof. Bharat Bhushan Sood graduated from the esteemed BITS Pilani with a degree in Mechanical Engineering, laying the foundation for his illustrious career. Subsequently, he pursued his M.B.A. from the prestigious Faculty of Management Studies (F.M.S.) at the University of Delhi, further augmenting his academic credentials. Renowned for his adeptness in revitalizing ailing companies, he is credited with orchestrating remarkable turnarounds, steering them towards growth and global prominence. His extensive travels have taken him to over 30 countries and cities, including Singapore, Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, and Malaysia, enriching his global perspective and fostering invaluable cross-cultural insights.
